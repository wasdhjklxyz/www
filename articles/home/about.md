## about

Hello! I'm uiop. I own three Thinkpads. I daily-drive a
[T420s](https://thinkwiki.de/T420s), my [X220](https://thinkwiki.de/X220) is
collecting dust, and my [T42](https://thinkwiki.de/T42) is completely
disassembled in a drawer somewhere.

I'm most proud of my [hobby OS](https://github.com/wasdhjklxyz/os) built
straight from the [AMD64 System Programming
Manual](https://docs.amd.com/v/u/en-US/24593_3.44_APM_Vol2) (I also have a
physical copy!!11!). It's the kind of project where I don't stop until *every*
bit of *every* data structure makes sense. I've spent countless hours reading
docs, debugging CPU resets/interrupts, and scribbling about whatever I'm
thinking in my notebook. These liminal, entracing periods of deep work that
stretch into the early AMs is what I live for.

Outside of software, I'm somewhere between making music that rarely gets
finished and being caught up in some rabbit hole that I won't care about when I
wake up the next day.
