# The Origin of uiop and wasdhjklxyz
<time>May 29, 2026</time>

The site name, wasdhjklxyz, came to me when I was creating my twitter account.
The most important aspect to me was that I wanted a username that could be its
own domain name. I was planning to post blogs and general personalish stuff so I
thought of "wasd" since I play computer games, and "hjkl" since I use Neovim
religiously. The TLD .xyz seemed appropriate since the entire string,
wasdhjklxyz, looks like random characters and I thought it was cool looking.

Now you might be wondering "wtf is a uiop?" After wasdhjklxyz, I had to come up
with a name/handle. I wanted something to look random like wasdhjklxyz, was
short, and easy to remember. I remembered from the show [Mr.
Robot](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mr._Robot), the protagonist named his fish
"qwerty" - which I thought was a cool pet name. If you look down at your
keyboard, you'll see that the next four keys after "qewrty" is "uiop." That's
where the name comes from!

Since I hear my friends say "uiop" in person sometimes, I figured I would add
how I pronounce "uiop" and "wasdhjklxyz." Obviously these aren't real English
words - there's no correct pronounciation, this is just what I say/think when I
read them.

/juːɒp/  
*yoo-op*
